Meet the Rumblebots Team!

The brains behind the bots.

Captains

Leading Rumblebots

Left to right:

Leonel O. Ramón Pagán, Ricardo Carrión López

Our captains are responsible for leading and guiding their respective teams, providing direction and support, and ensuring that the team members are working together effectively. They also act as a liaison between the teams and the club's leadership, ensuring that everyone is on the same page and working towards the same goals. The team captains' leadership skills and dedication are essential to our success as a robotics club. Without a great captain, no great team succeeds.

Design and Manufacturing

Designing for Success

Left to right:

Jorge Hernández Rivera, Kevin J. Castillo Moya, Cristian Matos, David O. Añeses Vera, Luis O. Del Río Montalvo, Full name not known, Bryan A. Reyes, Ricardo Antón, Keyshla Betancourt, Priscilla M. Santini Suarez, Ariana A. González Méndez

The team behind the design and construction of our robots plays a vital role in the development and performance of our machines. From the initial sketches to the final assembly, they are responsible for bringing our robots to life and ensuring that they are both visually appealing and competitive. Their expertise in design and manufacturing allows us to build robots that are both efficient and reliable. The team's hard work and dedication are essential for the success of our robots in competitions and for the overall success of our robotics team. With the Design and Manufacturing team's contributions, the team is one step closer to achieving our goals and greatness.

Logistics

Making it all possible

Left to right:

Alejandra Ayala Monserrate, Ariana Young, Yamilette Boscio Sanchez, Yarelis Hernández

From promoting the club and generating funds to organizing the teams' workflows the logistics team covers all the necessary team management to break new thresholds. They also handle the club's social media and ensure that the team's message is being communicated effectively. The logistics team's hard work and dedication are essential to our success as a robotics club and give the team the resources needed to innovate!

Software

Brain of the Robot

Left to right:

Juan Quintana, Jeanpiere I. Sanchez Felix, Yahid Díaz, Oscar Flores, Edimar Valentín Kery, Paola A. Rivera Martinez

The software team plays a crucial role in the functioning of our robots. They are responsible for programming the intelligence and decision-making abilities of our robots, turning them from mere machines into sophisticated machines that can navigate and perform tasks in a competitive environment. Without the hard work and dedication of the software team, our robots would not be able to compete at the level they do. They are the brains behind the machines, and their contributions are invaluable to our success as a robotics team.

Electronics

The Power to Win

Alfredo Vega Caraballo

The electronics team plays a critical role in the functioning of our robots. They are responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the electrical systems of our robots, including the power systems, sensors, and control systems. Their expertise in electronics allows our robots to have a wide range of capabilities and to perform complex tasks. Without the hard work and dedication of the electronics team, our robots would not be able to compete at the level they do. They are the heart of the machines, and their contributions are invaluable to our success as a robotics team.
