Who is Arsenal?

Arsenal is a 12-pound category fighting robot. Its the first robot ever developed by the team. The robot is remote controlled, and its weapon can be swapped between a vertical spinner and a horizontal one. Arsenal's purpose is to destroy the oponent, and it has everything it needs to do just that.

Who is La Cherry?

La Cherry is a 17 pound category robot that has been developed to aid in Arsenal's development. This robot is made for combat, the team decided to create another similar robot to serve as a sparring partner for Arsenal. Nevertheless, La Cherry has no weapon systems--unlike Arsenal. The absence of weapon systems for this bot is contrasted by its armor upgrades. Even though La Cherry has no attack weapons, the robot is a tank with increased frontal protection and a heavier chasis.

Chasis

Arsenal's chasis is made of Aluminum 6061, Ti metal, Nylon X, and polyurethane adhesive. These materials were chosen specifically for their lightweight characteristics.

Powertrain

The area of the plates that hold the Powertrain motor is of a 13.1 in by 7.87 in area. Inside lies the powertrain motor and gearbox. These components have been neatly accommodated using 3D printed components. These components, even though they increase the robot's weight, hold the powertrain and gearbox steady in place.

Weapon Systems

Arsenal has a modular weapon system. It can be exchanged from a verticle spinner or a horizontal one. The spinner rotates around an aluminum shaft. The shaft's side plates are made of aluminum, they were manufactured using a CNC machine. Under the shaft a roller system was added in order to prevent the weapon's ramp (which holds the spinner) from dragging and causing friction.
